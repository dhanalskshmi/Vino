Technical Support Engineer

Wall Township

Performs the day-day operations involved with technical project support and information technology related user/system support including, phone support, technical installations, technical requirements and distributed computer services.

Essential Duties & Responsibilities

  • Handle incoming calls requiring assistance with computer related problems.
  • Troubleshoot network related issues relating to operating systems, Microsoft Office, anti-virus, anti-spyware, and other network systems.
  • Establish and maintain network users, user environments, directories, and security.
  • Designs, specifies, configures, installs and maintains network hardware, software and telecommunications services.
  • Leads and/or supports Information Technology Division projects and supports requests including keeping tasks on tracking and reporting milestones.
  • Develops and implements policies and procedures related to network hardware and software acquisitions, use, support, security and backups.
  • Communicates with other departments to report and resolve software, hardware and operations problems.
  • Researches and evaluates new technologies. Develops and implements various documentation, procedures and standards.
  • Ensure that on site computer operating systems and anti-virus tools stay current.
  • Provide Support for access to all systems and maintenance of access control profiles on computer network.
  • Responds to the needs and questions of network users concerning their access to resources on the network and the operation of various software programs.
  • Document internal procedures.
  • Light Travel to branch sites may be required.
  • Ability and willingness to work a flexible work schedule including in addition to normal business hours in a 24 x 7 support environment.
  • Be on call after business hours.
  • Other duties as assigned

Job Specifications

  • Windows 7, 10
  • Microsoft Office (Word, Excel, Access, Outlook)
  • Windows Server 2008 & 2012, 2016, VMware, SQL.
  • Extensive knowledge of PC’s.
  • Extensive knowledge of Active Directory, DNS, DHCP and SAN/NAS
  • Networking Hardware (Switches, firewalls, routers)
  • Excellent interpersonal skills; ability to communicate effectively with all levels of personnel.
  • Demonstrated ability to identify and solve problems quickly and effectively.
  • Excellent organizational skills with the ability to prioritize and work effectively on multiple tasks.
  • Understanding of project management methodology.
  • Strong technical aptitude, desire and initiative.

Education/Experience:

  • >2-4 years of IT systems analysis and network experience.
  • >Bachelor’s degree preferred in Information Technology and or specific industry related certifications.
